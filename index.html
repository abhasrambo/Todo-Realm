What is Todo Book?

This app will help you to save all that you have planned at one place so when you need, you don't have to think or look for piece of paper where you noted down. You can plan your weekly grocery, Bills, Homework, schedule and so on.

How to use?

Once the app is launched you will land on category screen. In this screen you should organize by category example: Grocery, Bills, Homework.

You can add category by clicking the + icon on right side top of the screen.

Once you have added category you can click on any category and add all items that you want, example: you added category Grocery and clicked on it, now you can add items like milk, bread, Tea, Coffee. In this way you will never miss anything.
